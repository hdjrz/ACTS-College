Who We Are

Building Excellence
Through Faith & Technology

Asian Christian Technological School & Colleges (ACTS) is a premier institution in Taguig City dedicated to forming globally competitive graduates rooted in Christian faith. From Preschool to College, we provide quality and affordable education for every Filipino learner.

Our Vision

To be a leading Christian educational institution producing globally competitive, values-driven professionals who serve God, country, and community.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and accessible Christian education anchored in academic rigor, practical skills, and spiritual formation.

Core Values

Excellence · Integrity · Service · Faith · Innovation — the pillars of every ACTS graduate.

Admissions

Begin Your ACTS Journey

Enrollment is now open for S.Y. 2025–2026. Follow these four simple steps and become part of the ACTS family.

Step 1
Submit Application

Fill out the online or walk-in application form with your personal details and preferred program.

Step 2
Entrance Examination

Take the ACTS Entrance Examination at campus every Saturday. Results released in 3 working days.

Step 3
Submit Requirements

Present all required documents to the Registrar's Office for verification and processing.

Step 4
Pay & Enroll

Settle enrollment fees, receive your student ID, and officially join the ACTS family!

Required Documents

  • Original & Photocopy of Form 138 (Report Card)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Original & Photocopy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2×2 ID Photos — 4 pieces, white background
  • Medical Certificate (for incoming freshmen)
  • Long Brown Envelope

Frequently Asked Questions

ACTS offers a complete educational ladder from Preschool to College. Our tertiary programs include BS Information Technology, BS Computer Science, and BS Criminology. We also offer Senior High School (TVL-ICT Strand), Junior High School, Elementary, and Preschool levels — all DepEd and CHED recognized.

Tuition fees vary by program and level. ACTS is committed to providing affordable quality education. We offer flexible payment plans, CHED TES subsidies, academic scholarships, and the BLESS Project for underprivileged students. Contact our Registrar's Office at +63 (02) 8XXX-XXXX for the latest fee schedule.

Yes, we welcome transferees at all levels. Transferees must submit their Transcript of Records or Report Card from their previous school, along with other standard requirements. The admission process includes an assessment and interview. Contact our admissions office for assistance.

Program Overview

The BS Information Technology program equips students with competencies in systems administration, network management, web & mobile development, and enterprise IT solutions. Graduates are prepared for careers in the fast-evolving digital industry both locally and internationally.

Core Subjects
  • Network & Systems Administration
  • Web & Mobile Application Development

IT Support Specialist, Network Administrator, Web Developer, Systems Analyst, Database Administrator, Mobile App Developer, IT Project Manager

Requirements

Completed Senior High School (any strand), passed the ACTS Entrance Examination, and submitted all required enrollment documents.

BS Criminology
4-Year Course · CHED Recognized
Program Overview

The BS Criminology program prepares students for careers in law enforcement, criminal justice, forensics, and public safety. The curriculum integrates board exam review components to help graduates pass the Criminologist Licensure Examination.

Core Subjects
  • Criminalistics & Forensic Science
  • Law Enforcement Administration
  • Criminal Law & Jurisprudence
  • Crime Detection & Investigation
  • Correctional Administration
  • Sociology of Crimes & Ethics
  • Internship & Board Exam Review
Career Opportunities

Police Officer (PNP), Forensic Scientist, Criminal Investigator, Corrections Officer, Security Consultant, Intelligence Analyst, Probation Officer

Requirements

Completed Senior High School (any strand), passed the ACTS Entrance Examination, medical certificate, and submitted all required enrollment documents.

TVL — ICT Strand
Senior High School · DepEd Recognized
Program Overview

The TVL-ICT strand is a technology-focused Senior High School track that prepares Grade 11–12 learners for college or immediate employment in the ICT industry. Students gain practical skills in programming, graphic design, and multimedia production.

Core Subjects
  • Programming Fundamentals (Java/Python)
  • Graphic Design & Multimedia Arts
  • Computer Hardware Servicing
  • Animation & Digital Illustration
  • Web Development (HTML/CSS/JS)
  • Work Immersion / OJT
Certifications

Graduates are eligible for CSS NC II (Computer Systems Servicing National Certificate II) from TESDA, giving them an immediate employment advantage.

Requirements

Completed Junior High School (Grade 10), Form 138 / Report Card, PSA Birth Certificate, and Good Moral Character certificate.

Junior High School
Grades 7–10 · DepEd K–12 Curriculum
Program Overview

ACTS Junior High School provides a holistic K–12 education that integrates the DepEd standard curriculum with Christian values, 21st-century skills, and technology exposure. Students build a strong academic foundation while developing moral character.

Preschool & Elementary
Nursery to Grade 6 · DepEd Recognized
Program Overview

ACTS Preschool & Elementary nurtures young hearts and minds in a safe, Christ-centered environment. Our program blends play-based early learning with structured academics, character formation, and parent partnership to build strong foundations for lifelong success.

Key Learning Areas
  • Reading, Writing & Language Development
  • Mathematics & Science Exploration
  • Christian Living & Bible Stories
  • Art, Music & Physical Education
  • Computer Literacy (Grades 3–6)
